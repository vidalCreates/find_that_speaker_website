Features

FIND THAT SPEAKER the android app

Step 1

Tap record to capture the person's voice for matching. Be sure to capture ten seconds of clear audio for the best results.

Step 2

Wait for the app to match the recording to the speaker

Step 3

Once a match is found, their biography will be displayed

Uses of the application

"Find that speaker" goes beyond identifying speakers in the context of presentations, it can also be used for the following !

  • An Authentication system

    for gaining access to the building of your company

  • Convenient feature of a smart house

    appliances could only respond to certain speakers' voices and carry out certain commands.

  • Integrated into a phone's panic button

    When the phone's panic button is pressed, the audio samples captured can be sent to "Find that speaker" and any known speaker in vicinity can then be identified.
